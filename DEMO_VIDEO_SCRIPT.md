### **0:00 - 0:15 | Introduction & Hook**

**(Visual: Upbeat music, quick cuts of beautiful travel destinations, then a shot of someone looking bored at their desk.)**

### **0:15 - 0:30 | The Problem**

**(Visual: Show generic travel planning websites, then a person struggling with photo editing software.)**

**Narrator:** "Planning a trip can be overwhelming, and imagining yourself in those dream destinations is often left to complex photo editing. Traditional travel sites lack that personal touch, and creating fun travel photos usually requires serious graphic design skills. We wanted to make travel inspiration and personalization effortless."

### **0:30 - 1:15 | Features Overview & Destination Explorer Demo**

**(Visual: Transition to the TravelSnap homepage. Highlight "Explore Destinations" button.)**

**Narrator:** "TravelSnap solves this with two core features. First, our **Destination Explorer**! Click 'Explore Destinations' and you're greeted with a vibrant grid of popular holiday spots."

**(Visual: Navigate to Destinations page. Show scrolling through cards for Paris, Tokyo, Santorini. Click on Paris card, show the alert with details.)**

**Narrator:** "Each card gives you a glimpse, and a quick tap reveals key facts, must-visit places, and exciting activities. From the romantic streets of Paris to the bustling energy of Tokyo, TravelSnap helps you discover your next adventure."

### **1:15 - 2:30 | AI Photo Placement Demo**

**(Visual: Transition to the "Create Travel Photo" section. Show user uploading an image, selecting a landmark from the dropdown.)**

**Narrator:** "But here's where the magic happens: our **AI Photo Placement**! Simply upload a picture of yourself – a selfie, a fun pose, anything you like."

**(Visual: Show the file upload process, then selecting 'Eiffel Tower' from the dropdown.)**

**Narrator:** "Then, choose your dream landmark. Let's pick the iconic Eiffel Tower."

**(Visual: Click "Generate Travel Photo." Show the loading indicator briefly. Then, reveal the generated image with the user seamlessly placed in front of the Eiffel Tower, in a fun pose.)**

**Narrator:** "With a single click, our AI gets to work. And just like that... *voilà!* You're now in Paris! Our intelligent AI seamlessly integrates you into the scene, matching lighting and perspective, creating your very own personalized travel postcard. It's playful, it's fun, and it's instantly shareable!"

**(Visual: Briefly show another example with a different landmark like Times Square.)**

**Narrator:** "Imagine yourself in Times Square, or soaking up the sun in Santorini – the possibilities are endless!"

**(Visual: Quick text overlay: "Challenge: AI Integration Complexity" or "Challenge: Real-time Processing")**
